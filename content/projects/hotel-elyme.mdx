## Overview

Hotel Elyme needed a website that could carry the atmosphere of a boutique stay while making practical decisions simple. The challenge was to keep the experience warm and tactile without slowing down the path to booking.

## Challenge

The old site separated image, information and conversion. Visitors could sense the place, but the experience asked them to work too hard to understand rooms, location and availability.

<BeforeAfter before="/images/atelier-hero.png" after="/images/project-hotel-elyme.png" beforeAlt="Earlier fragmented presentation" afterAlt="Refined hospitality website direction" />

## Objectives

- Build a more cinematic first impression.
- Clarify the room and experience hierarchy.
- Keep booking visible without making it aggressive.
- Create modular content blocks for seasonal offers.

## Approach

The design system uses editorial pacing, restrained motion and concise wayfinding. Every transition is small enough to feel premium and fast, but present enough to guide attention.

<CaseImage src="/images/project-hotel-elyme.png" alt="Hotel Elyme hospitality website mockup" />

## Results

The redesigned presence helped the hotel communicate its difference earlier in the journey while making the commercial path shorter and more confident.

<Quote>The interface became quieter, but the offer became easier to feel.</Quote>

## Technologies

Next.js, Framer Motion, responsive art direction, semantic HTML and Cloudflare delivery.
