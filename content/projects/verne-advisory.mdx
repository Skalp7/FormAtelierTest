## Overview

Verne Advisory had grown through relationships and reputation. The new presence needed to translate that trust into a public system: clear services, credible proof and lightweight internal workflows.

## Challenge

The practice did not need a loud brand. It needed a structure that made expertise easier to evaluate and a content model that could support future resources, articles and client references.

## Objectives

- Define a calm, credible positioning system.
- Create service pages that can scale later.
- Connect enquiries to practical internal workflows.
- Prepare the site for future resources and case studies.

<CaseImage src="/images/project-verne-advisory.png" alt="Verne Advisory website and operations mockup" />

## Approach

We mapped the business around decision moments: what a prospect needs to know, what proof matters and what action should follow. The interface is deliberately plain in the right places so the thinking can become the signal.

<VideoEmbed title="Placeholder strategy walkthrough" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" />

## Results

Verne Advisory gained a public presence that feels established without becoming heavy. The foundation can now grow into a resource library, deeper service pages and a private client portal.

## Technologies

Next.js, MDX, GitHub-based content workflow, Cloudflare Pages and AI-assisted production reviews.
